About

At Pooped Scoop, we provide reliable, affordable dog poop removal services for residential and commercial properties. Based in Ellenton, we’re dedicated to keeping your yard clean, safe, and odor-free — so you can enjoy your outdoor space without the mess. Whether you need weekly poop scooping, one-time cleanups, or pet waste station servicing, we’ve got you covered. Trust a local, professional poop scooper who truly cares about your pets and your property!

Services

  • Residential Service

    We offer reliable and affordable dog poop cleanup for your home. Whether it’s a one-time visit or routine weekly service, we’ll keep your yard clean, safe, and ready to enjoy. Let us handle the dirty work so you don’t have to!

  • Commercial Service

    We provide professional pet waste removal for apartments, HOAs, parks, and commercial properties. Keep your outdoor spaces clean, sanitary, and welcoming with reliable service tailored to your property's needs. We also offer pet waste station maintenance and installation.

  • Yard Deodorizing

    Say goodbye to lingering pet odors! Our yard deodorizing service uses pet-safe, eco-friendly products to eliminate smells and leave your outdoor space smelling fresh and clean. Perfect for homes, kennels, and high-traffic pet areas.

  • Cat Litter Cleanup

    We take the hassle out of maintaining your cat’s litter box. Whether it’s a one-time deep clean or regular service, we’ll scoop, sanitize, and refresh your litter boxes so your home stays clean and odor-free. Perfect for busy pet owners or seniors needing a hand!

  • Additional Services

    We offer more than just cleanup! Count on us for dependable dog walking, pet sitting, food and water refills, and even medication administration. Whether you’re at work, on vacation, or just need an extra hand, we’re here to give your pets the love and care they deserve — right in the comfort of their home.
